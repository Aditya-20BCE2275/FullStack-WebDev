Vetiver Essential Oil

Also known as khus oil, Vetiver essential oil is extracted from the vetiver plant that is native to India. This oil is quite fragrant and provides a number of benefits like soothes and enhances mental alertness, enhancing sleep quality, reducing anxiety, protecting from ticks, reducing the symptoms of ADHD, high antioxidant properties etc.

Palmarosa Essential Oil

Palmarosa oil is useful in combating anxiety, mood swings, grief, anger. Other than the emotional impacts, Palmarosa essential oil also helps relieve the symptoms of sinusitis, cystitis, Urinary Tract Infection, Acne, Pimples, Fungal infections, fatigue, stress, sore muscles, restlessness and insect bites.

Lemongrass Essential Oil

This oil is often used in aromatherapy and is a known to have several benefits to the users. It helps relieve stress, induce a calm sleep, relieve headaches, indigestion, inflammation, muscle aches, acne, anxiety, athlete's foot and dandruff. Other than this, Lemongrass essence also acts as mosquito and insect repellant. Research also indicates that lemongrass essential oil contains compounds which give it analgesic, antiviral, antidepressant, antimicrobial, antioxidant and sedative qualities as well.

Geranium Essential Oil

This oil is majorly used for perfumes and cosmetics, however, scientific research has shown that Geranium essential oils also imbibe a lot of health benefits. Geranium essential oils are considered to have astringent, antioxidant, antimicrobial, antibacterial and anti-inflammatory properties. For the same reason, this oil can be used to treat conditions like acne, edema, dermatitis, inflammation, nasal vestibulitis, bacterial infections, neurodegenerative diseases, menopause, stress, anxiety, pains and aches, allergies, diabetes etc.

Chamomile Essential Oil

Chamomile oil has been used for years by ancient Greeks, Romans and Egyptians for curing conditions such as digestive upset, indigestion, nausea, gas, wound healing, ulcers, sores, anxiety, depression, eczema, allergies etc. Chamomile Oil can also be used to get relief from inflammations, back pain, neuralgia and arthritis. This oil also helps promote sleep. Research also established that Chamomile extracts have anti-cancer abilities. When cancer cells were treated with chamomile extracts, the cancer cells died.
